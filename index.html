Frequently asked questions

Who is this book for?

This book is for anyone who wants to use options strategically to protect their finances and invest successfully for the long term. It is ideal for beginners looking for a structured entry into options trading, and for advanced investors who want to deepen their knowledge and build a systematic approach. It is not intended for short-term traders or readers looking for quick profits without effort.

Do I need prior knowledge to understand the book?

No. The book explains all the basics of options trading from scratch and guides you through the topic step by step. Many practical examples make the concepts easy to grasp. Basic knowledge of stocks and the stock market is assumed.

What are the benefits of the bonus material?

The bonus material includes practical templates for your trading journal, checklists for your trading strategies, and helpful tools for selecting strategies. These resources help you apply the concepts from the book faster and make it easier to get started with options trading.

How much capital do I need to start trading options?

The book recommends starting with at least €5,000 to €10,000 to build meaningful positions. This allows for sufficient diversification and the use of risk-limited strategies. Only invest capital you do not need for daily living expenses.

Can I buy the book as an e-book?

The book is currently available only as a paperback. An e-book version is in the works—check our website for updates.
