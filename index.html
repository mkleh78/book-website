Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this book suitable for?

This book is aimed at anyone who wants to use options strategically to secure their finances and invest successfully over the long term. It's ideal for beginners seeking a structured introduction to options trading, but also for advanced investors who want to deepen their knowledge and develop a systematic approach. However, it's not suitable for short-term oriented traders or readers looking for quick profits without effort.

Do I need prior knowledge to understand the book?

No, the book explains all fundamentals of options trading from the beginning and introduces you to the subject step by step. Many practical examples have been integrated to make the concepts clear. However, basic knowledge of stocks and stock trading is assumed.

What advantages does the bonus material offer?

The bonus material includes practical templates for your trading journal, checklists for your trading strategies, and helpful tools for strategy selection. These materials help you implement the concepts presented in the book more quickly in practice and make it easier to get started with options trading.

How much capital do I need to start trading options?

The book recommends starting with at least €5,000 to €10,000 to be able to build meaningful positions. This enables sufficient diversification and the application of risk-limited strategies. It's important that you only use capital that you don't need for your daily living expenses.

Can I purchase the book as an e-book?

Currently, the book is only available as a paperback. An e-book version is in planning. Visit our website for updates on this topic.
